SOCIETY FOR HUMAN RESOURCE MANAGEMENT (SHRM)

The Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM) is a professional organization dedicated to advancing the HR profession and its members through education, research, advocacy, and networking opportunities. With over 300,000 members in 165 countries, SHRM provides resources and tools that help HR professionals stay updated on industry news, trends, and best practices. The organization offers certification programs, access to HR conferences and events, and publishes instructional materials to help members develop their skills and knowledge. SHRM also advocates for HR policies and legislation that promote workplace fairness and diversity.

SHRM ALIGNED PROGRAM

A SHRM aligned program is an educational course designed by subject matter experts to meet the standards set forth by the Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM), the premier organization for HR professionals. These programs are designed to support HR professionals in their professional development and to ensure that attendees are equipped with up-to-date knowledge and skills required for their respective roles in HR. A SHRM aligned program is designed to provide learners with a comprehensive and relevant curriculum, which is aligned with the current trends and best practices in HR management.
