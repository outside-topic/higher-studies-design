Master of Business Administration (MBA)

If you are interested in pursuing a Master's in Business Administration (MBA) degree, but you don't have the time or resources to attend a traditional program, you might consider a self-study based program. Self-study online is a flexible and convenient way to learn the core concepts and skills of business administration, such as accounting, finance, marketing, strategy, leadership, Entrepreneurship, Business Administration etc.

You can study at your own pace, from anywhere in the world, and choose from 32 specialized areas. Whether you want to start your own business, move into a leadership role, or switch industries, this MBA degree can help you get there. This recognized credential will boost your resume and salary potential.
