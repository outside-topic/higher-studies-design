Program Overview

Empowering Global Professionals

World Academy is offering the MBA program which is designed and provided to professionals who wants to equip themselves with skills, knowledge and experience needed to succeed in the global business environment.

We aim in develop business leaders with a strategic perspective, analytical skills, and an entrepreneurial mindset. The application-based curriculum will teach participants to think critically, manage complex projects, and communicate effectively with diverse stakeholders. Graduates of the program are prepared to take on leadership roles in a range of industries and sectors, from finance and consulting to technology and healthcare

World Academy is the global partner of European International University (EIU-Paris), which is globally acknowledged for its academic excellence and ensures that participants are provided with a conducive learning experience that fosters knowledge acquisition and skills development. EIU-Paris is ranked 55 among all the universities in 2023 according to the CEO world Magazine. In addition, EIU-Paris has a QS STAR rating of 5 stars for online learning and teaching quality and its graduates can obtain US and Canadian equivalency for their degrees. EIU-Paris graduates also have direct access to UK dual awards/qualifications.

How to Apply

Application & Admission procedure at a Glance

Follow these simple steps from selecting your desired MBA specialization to celebrating your graduation with us.

01

Select your program

Select any MBA from our wide variety of Specializations and submit the required documents via email to apply.

02

Check Eligibility

The required documents submitted by candidates will be assessed by the Admission Committee to determine eligibility for the program along with the partial scholarship.

03

Confirmation Email

An email confirmation will be sent regarding the decision of the admission committee.

04

Offer Letter & Invoice

Selected Candidates will receive an offer letter for the program applied, along with the invoice for the first payment.

05

Make Payment

Pay the invoice amount and wait for the confirmation. Upon confirmation, new participants' profile will be activated on the portal within 24hrs.

06

Receive Acceptance Letter

From the day of payment, participants will get the Acceptance Letter within 7-10 working days.

07

Academic Orientation

The Virtual orientation will be conducted as soon as the enrollment is completed within 5 to 7 working days.

08

Start the Course

After orientation participants will be fully prepared to begin the program. Participants will also be connected with our team for any assistance.

09

Graduation Requirements

To earn a degree participants must complete all ECT reserved for the program, pass on all the assignments and the ARP (Action Research Paper) with a minimum 2.5 cumulative GPA and fulfill all financial obligations.

10

Certificate

Official Certificate and Transcript (Electronic and Printed version) shall be provided after successful completion of the program.

11

Convocation

After successful completion of the program, graduates can attend the convocation ceremony which requires separate registration. Graduates are encouraged but it is not mandatory.

Required Documents

Application Requirement

Please ensure you have the following documents ready in PDF or Image format before starting your application process.

01

Certificates

Education Certificate (of Bachelor’s degree/graduate Diploma) & transcripts (PDF/image format).

02

Latest CV / Resume

Latest CV / Resume (PDF file format). Candidates need to submit a latest and complete CV including all the academic, personal and professional information.

03

Passport Sized Photo

Passport Sized Photo (JPG or PNG file format). A passport sized/clear photo is needed for the application form.

04

Reference Letter(s)

Recommenders should provide their impressions of your intellectual ability, research or professional skills, character, past work quality, and potential for future scholarship.

05

Identification Card

Passport copy (image format) or National id card copy (PDF/image format).

06

Personal Statement

A minimum 500-word statement explaining how the program aligns with your career goals, what you hope to gain from it at this stage, and how you envision your career progressing over the next five years.
